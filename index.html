About NU Players

Star Players:

National University dominated the season with Bella Belen winning her third MVP award. She led the team with 96.23 statistical points, making her one of the most decorated players in UAAP history.

Setter Lams Lamina played a crucial role in orchestrating NU’s offense, delivering 20 excellent sets in the finals. Meanwhile, Shaira Jardio anchored the defense with **28 excellent receptions and 18 digs.

NU's Championship Legacy

The Lady Bulldogs secured their second consecutive championship, proving their dominance in UAAP volleyball. Their teamwork and strategic plays led them to a sweep victory over La Salle in the finals.

UAAP Season 87 Women's Volleyball Final

Finals Game 2 Best of Three Series

The UAAP Season 87 Women's Volleyball Final showcased an intense battle between the top teams in the league. The championship match was held at the Mall of Asia Arena, where fans gathered to witness the thrilling showdown.

NU secured a straight-set victory (25-19, 25-18, 25-19) over La Salle, marking their third championship in four seasons.


Highlights of the Match

Key moments and players, May 2025

NU’s defense was impenetrable, with Jardio contributing 28 excellent receptions and 18 digs, shutting down La Salle’s offense. Despite Angel Canino’s 22-point effort for DLSU, NU’s aggressive attacks and strategic plays overwhelmed the Lady Spikers, marking their third championship in four seasons and solidifying their dominance in UAAP volleyball.
